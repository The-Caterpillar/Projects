Security & Help

1. PHYSIOT Clinical Privacy Policy

PHYSIOT Clinical greatly values the privacy of customers and all persons from whom personal information is collected by or shared with PHYSIOT Clinical. To transparently demonstrate PHYSIOT Clinical’s commitment to maintaining the highest level of security and privacy for our clients and visitors to our Site, we outline our Privacy Policy herein. PHYSIOT Clinical standards for privacy protection are set forth to be in accordance with privacy regulations globally, including the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), local state regulations and the privacy and confidentiality requirements of International Conference on Harmonization (ICH) Good Clinical Practice (GCP). To monitor, review, and support adherence to the PHYSIOT Clinical Privacy Policy, we have established an internal Security Team, dedicated to the development, implementation, and maintenance of Security and Privacy Policies to best protect personal information.

1.1 Conditional Use of this Site

An individual’s access to and use of the Site and its contents are subject to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy and all applicable laws. By accessing and using the Site, Site users accept and agree to these terms and conditions without any limitation or qualification. Use of the Site is also subject to the Terms of Use posted on the Site (“Terms of Use”).

1.2 Type of Personal Information Collected

The purpose of this Policy is to protect all personal information received or gathered by PHYSIOT Clinical. This applies to patients, healthcare professionals, customers, suppliers, vendors, business partners, and all other persons from which personal information is collected. PHYSIOT Clinical takes the stance of minimizing unnecessary collection and sharing of any personal information.

1.3 Clinical/Medical and Health Professional Information

All medical information collected from patients or shared with PHYSIOT Clinical by medical professionals, or investigators, is performed under the contract. PHYSIOT Clinical considers the entity generating protected healthcare information to be ultimately responsible for how healthcare information is shared and processed. Data may be transferred to sponsors of clinical trials, investigators of clinical trials, other contracted organizations. All data collected by PHYSIOT Clinical is processed in accordance with Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) and GCP. Moreover, whenever possible patient data will be de-identified. PHYSIOT Clinical may obtain and analyze professional information from health care professionals for the purpose of assisting with identification and evaluation of investigators. PHYSIOT Clinical may use contact information for the purpose of inviting investigators to communicate with sponsors and participate in research. We will use public sources, internal databases, and referrals to obtain information. Financial information for payment of services may also be processed. PHYSIOT Clinical utilizes surveys to collect information on patients that are currently enrolled in, or have completed, clinical trials or expanded access programs (EAPs). From these surveys, PHYSIOT Clinical may generate protected information that includes, but is not limited to, names, emails, clinical diagnoses, and IP addresses. PHYSIOT Clinical only uses qualified survey providers, with HIPAA compliant security measures. All data generated from the surveys is stored in a password protected and encrypted fashion. Data is only sent out to third parties as required for data analysis, And data is de-identified prior to being sent out unless specific permission to share PHI is granted by the patient or guardian. All survey patrons receive and sign an informed consent prior to beginning the survey.

1.4 Employee Data

Physiot Clinical collects personal information from its employees. This information may include but is not limited to, contact details, professional qualifications, employment history, background checks, payroll, tax information, and other human resource data. This is performed in accordance with standard business operations. PHYSIOT Clinical policy is not to share or sell employee information without specific permission being granted.

1.5 PHYSIOT Clinical Site Patrons

Due to our obligation to collect minimal personal information, Physiot Clinical does not store any “cookies” information from Site patrons. Site patrons may opt to contact PHYSIOT Clinical via the “Contact Us” link. In this case, email and any other information the patron chooses to provide may be collected. PHYSIOT Clinical will not be share information unless the individual agrees to conditions and terms of information sharing. The Physiot Clinical Site may contain links to other websites that we do not own or operate. We do not control, recommend, or endorse and are not responsible for these websites or their content, products, services or privacy policies. Downloading material from certain websites may risk infringing intellectual property rights or introducing viruses into your system. When you leave the PHYSIOT Clinical Site, you should read the privacy policies and terms of these other websites. You should also independently assess the authenticity of any website which appears or claims that it is one of our websites (including those linked to through an email). PHYSIOT Clinical may use the information collected as defined herein to comply with legal and regulatory responsibilities, policies, and for administrative purposes.

1.6 Internal and External Sharing of Personal Information

As part of PHYSIOT Clinical’s effort to minimize data collection and sharing, we strive to only collect and share information when required for business purposes. The affiliates with whom data may be shared include sponsors of clinical trials, investigators of clinical trials, business associates, and other third parties. Access to information is restricted to appropriate staff, both internally and externally. PHYSIOT Clinical does not sell or trade personal information. PHYSIOT Clinical will divulge information only to appropriate persons as required by law enforcement or judicial authorities as part of an investigation. PHYSIOT Clinical may share information with a buyer in the instance of a sale or transfer of PHYSIOT Clinical or its assets. Companies working as agents of PHYSIOT Clinical are required to sign a confidentiality agreement, in which they commit to only process personal information for agreed upon purposes, and must adhere to organizational and security safeguards. PHYSIOT Clinical is a provider of clinical supply and management services globally and may share information across international borders. PHYSIOT Clinical performs international sharing and hosting of data in accordance with appropriate regulatory processes were legally mandated.

1.7 Security

All PHYSIOT Clinical policies which utilize appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect personal information from unauthorized use, disclosure, alteration or destruction, and to adhere to required regulations. This includes limiting access to essential personnel, minimizing data sharing, and using password protection. However, PHYSIOT Clinical cannot guarantee the security of such information. To the fullest extent permitted by law, PHYSIOT Clinical disclaims all liability and responsibility for any damages you may suffer due to any loss, unauthorized access, misuse or alteration of any information you submit to our Site.

1.8 Data Integrity and Usage

PHYSIOT Clinical takes all reasonable precautions to ensure that information is accurate, complete, and up to date. Information is only to be used for purposes stated during information collection unless the other use is explicitly specified and consented to by the individual. PHYSIOT Clinical will take all steps to correct, amend, or delete information that does not meet the aforementioned criteria, except when in conflict with regulations. Data will be retained for durations required by appropriate regulations.

1.9 Reservation of Rights

PHYSIOT Clinical reserves the right to share personal information as defined in our contracts, as authorized by law, or in response to requests by government authorities.

1.10 Changes to Privacy Policy

The PHYSIOT Clinical Privacy Policy may be amended at the discretion of PHYSIOT Clinical, without notice as deemed appropriate. Amendments will be posted on the Site. This policy is not a contract and does not create any legal rights or obligations.
